# NASA-CDSS-Framework

## Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) for Long-Duration Spaceflight

This repository is a collaborative space for exploring the design and development of an AI-enabled Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) for long-duration human spaceflight.

The project aims to bring together researchers, engineers, clinicians, AI practitioners, and policy experts interested in advancing astronaut health support systems.

## Vision

This repository serves as a coordination and early design space for the CDSS initiative, with governance-first scoping and modular evolution into dedicated layer-specific repositories over time. The goal is to develop a modular, trustworthy, and human-centered CDSS architecture capable of supporting astronaut health monitoring, risk assessment, decision support, and mission planning during long-duration space missions.
